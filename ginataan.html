Ginataan

Ginataan refers to Filipino dishes cooked in coconut milk. It can be savory or sweet and often includes vegetables, seafood, or meat.

Ingredients

  • Coconut milk
  • Vegetables or seafood
  • Garlic and onion
  • Ginger

Cooking Method

Sauté aromatics, add coconut milk, then simmer ingredients gently until cooked and flavorful.
