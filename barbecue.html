Filipino Barbecue

Filipino-style barbecue is sweet, smoky, and savory, usually marinated in soy sauce, banana ketchup, and sugar.

Ingredients

  • Pork or chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Banana ketchup
  • Sugar and garlic

Cooking Method

Marinate meat, skewer, then grill while basting until caramelized.
