Nilagang Baka

Nilagang Baka is a comforting Filipino beef soup made with tender meat, corn, potatoes, and cabbage.

Ingredients

  • Beef shank
  • Corn
  • Potatoes
  • Cabbage

Cooking Method

Boil beef until tender, add vegetables, and season lightly.
